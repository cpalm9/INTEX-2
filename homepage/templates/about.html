Founder: Johan Anderson

The Family Oriented Music Organization is a family owned music store that’s been operating in Utah Valley since 1952. They have been profitable but are always looking for ways to improve their business.


Johan Anderson knows he needs a top-quality information system to run his business, and in this document he shares with us his thoughts on the business processes that support the company. Johan started by sharing his vision of FOMO:



At the Family Oriented Music Organization, we are commited to providing the best experience for all of our customers. In order to ensure that our website is easily accessible to all, we've ensured that all images are enabled with "alt" text and our homepage links stand out clearly from surround text. In addition to this, we've made visible labels outside of text boxes (see our "Contact" page) and organized our pages using hierarchical headings (see our "FAQs" page). If there is anything else we can do to make your FOMO experience more enjoyable, please do not hesitate to contact us!



“The Family Oriented Music Organization is a store that aims to satisfy the musical needs of musicians and budding musicians in the area. We provide instruments, accessories, repairs, sheet music, and training opportunities. Our store has four main revenue streams: sales, rentals, venue hosting and services.” - Johan Anderson
