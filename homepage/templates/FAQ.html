Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it better to start with a new or used instrument?
A: It really just depends on your budget. For beginners a used insrument would be just fine.
Q: Does FOMO allow for financing on instrument purchases?
A: Of course we do. We are always looking out for ways to get more music in the hands of our customers.
Q: Does FOMO provide money back guarantees on their instruments in case of freak accidents?
A: Yes, we provide warranties on all our instrument purchases and some of out other items as well. Each is specific to the purchase.
Q: Does FOMO provide Tuning?
A: Bring it in! We like a challenge. We will tune your stringed instruments of all shapes and sizes.
Q: Does FOMO ship items out of state?
A: Of course we do. If you need something shipped feel free to call us at 555-555-5555.
Q: How do I sign up for lessons with a teacher?
A: The easiest way to sign up is to stop in. We can give you a "tour", eplain the pricing and policies, find an available time that works for everyone, get you registered and paid all in one trip. You can also call us 555-555-5555 or email us at lessons@fomo.com to get the process started.
